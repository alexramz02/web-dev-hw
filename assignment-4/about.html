About Robert Frost

Robert Frost was born in San Francisco in 1874. He wasn't very well known for the first 40 years of his life. It wasn't until he came back to the United States at the beginning of World War one that people started to discover who is was. He won four Pulitzer prizes. He was a special guest at John F Kennedy's inaugration. He then became the unofficial "poet laureate" of the United States. He then passed away in 1963 due to complications during his prostate surgery.
